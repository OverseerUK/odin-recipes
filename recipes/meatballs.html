Meatballs

Juicy tomato meatballs

This lovely meatball recipe is super easy to make and very tasty with a meaty, rich sauce.

Ingredients

  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • 2 medium onions, finely chopped
  • 2 carrots, trimmed and finely chopped
  • 2 celery sticks, finely chopped
  • 2 garlic cloves finely chopped
  • 2-3 sprigs rosemary leaves picked and finely chopped
  • 500g beef meatballs

    • For the tomato sauce

  • 2 x 400g tins plum tomatoes
  • small pack basil leaves picked and finely chopped
  • 1 tsp dried oregano
  • 2 fresh bay leaves
  • 2 tbsp tomato purée
  • 1 beef stock cube

    • To season and serve

  • 400g spaghetti
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Method

  1. Put a large saucepan on a medium/high heat and add 1 tbsp olive oil.
  2. Add 500g beef meatballs and cook for 2 mins, then roll the meatballs over to cook the other side for 2 mins until browned all over, then remove from the saucepan and set aside.
  3. Reduce the heat and add the 2 onions, 2 carrots, 2 celery sticks, 2 garlic cloves and the leaves from 2-3 sprigs rosemary, all finely chopped, then fry for 10 mins. Stir the veg often until it softens.
  4. Add 2 tins plum tomatoes, the finely chopped leaves from small pack basil, 1 tsp dried oregano, 2 bay leaves, 2 tbsp tomato purée, 1 beef stock cube. Stir with a wooden spoon, breaking up the plum tomatoes.
  5. Bring to the boil, reduce to a gentle simmer, add back your meatballs and cover with a lid. Cook for 1 hr 15 mins stirring occasionally, until you have a rich, thick sauce.
  6. When the meatballs are nearly finished, cook 400g spaghetti following the pack instructions.
  7. Drain the spaghetti and either stir into the sauce, or serve the sauce on top.
